Privacy Policy — FinanceApp

Last Updated: July 17, 2026

1. Introduction

FinanceApp (Ali Sodikin) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application FinanceApp (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • PIN (hashed using SHA-256 with salt)
  • Account creation date

2.2 Financial Data

The App allows you to track your personal finances. Data you input includes:

  • Transaction details (amount, date, category, description)
  • Wallet/account information
  • Budget goals and savings targets
  • Receipt images (if using scan feature with Google ML Kit)

2.3 Device Information

We may collect limited device information for app functionality and analytics:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Crash logs (for bug fixes)

2.4 Payment Information

For Pro subscription purchases, we use Google Play Billing in conjunction with RevenueCat for subscription management. We do NOT store credit card information — Google handles all payment processing securely, while RevenueCat processes subscription status and entitlement data only..

2.5 Chatbot Interaction Data

Questions submitted to our Money IQ chatbot assistant that our system cannot process (due to being outside the app's scope, or due to insufficient matching data) may be stored anonymously — without any user identifier or account link — to help us improve the chatbot's accuracy over time. These records are automatically excluded from your account data and cannot be traced back to you. This data is retained for up to 30 days and periodically purged thereafter.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Cloud Storage (Supabase)

Your data is stored in Supabase's secure PostgreSQL database, hosted in the South Asia (Mumbai, India) region. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+.

3.2 PIN Security

Your PIN is hashed using SHA-256 with a random salt using Web Crypto API. We never store your PIN in plain text.

3.3 Access Control

We use Row-Level Security (RLS) in our database. You can only access your own data. Other users cannot view your financial information.

3.4 Data Encryption

All data in transit to our servers is encrypted using TLS. Your device-to-server communication is secure.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for:

  • Providing app functionality (tracking transactions, budgeting, reports)
  • Processing subscription payments
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Compliance with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Share your financial data with third parties (except Google for payment processing)
  • Use your data for marketing or advertising
  • Sell your data to any entity

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Supabase

Supabase (https://supabase.com) is our backend provider. Review their privacy policy at: https://supabase.com/privacy

5.2 Google ML Kit

If you use the receipt scan feature, Google ML Kit processes images on your device (not sent to Google servers). See: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/privacy-and-security

5.3 Google Play Billing

For subscription payments. Google handles all payment data. Review Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.4 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat (https://www.revenuecat.com) to manage and process subscription purchases. RevenueCat receives your app user identifier, subscription status, and purchase event data to verify and sync your Pro subscription status. Review their privacy policy at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time, which will remove all your personal data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights (Indonesia GDPR-like)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise these rights, contact: support@finance-app.pro

8. Children's Privacy

FinanceApp is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes means you accept the new policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Email: support@finance-app.pro
  • Email: team@finance-app.pro
  • Address: Pemalang, Central Java, Indonesia

11. How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

  1. Send an email to support@finance-app.pro
  2. Use subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include your registered email address in the message

We will process your request and delete the following data within 30 days: your profile, transactions, wallets, budgets, savings goals, and all personal information associated with your account. We may retain anonymized, non-identifiable statistics for legal or audit purposes only.

You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
